This Privacy Policy governs the processing of personal data by Abex Excelencia Robótica, S.L. (hereinafter, Abex) in the context of the Da Vinci Robotic Surgery Training Platform for medical residents (hereinafter, the Platform), in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and Spanish Organic Law 3/2018 (LOPDGDD).

1. Identity of the Data Controller

Abex Excelencia Robótica, S.L.
Tax ID (CIF): B87406799
Privacy contact email: privacy@abexsl.es

2. Purpose of Processing and Legal Basis

According to Article 13.1.c) of the GDPR, personal data shall be processed for the following purposes:

  • Management of the user's participation in the training program, including registration, progress tracking, and validation of training activities.
  • Management of users and roles within the Platform (resident, tutor, CSM, professor, participant, administrator).
  • Internal analysis and statistical reporting for program evaluation and improvement.
  • Compliance with legal or contractual obligations related to the training agreement between the resident and the hospital entity.

The legal basis for processing is the user's consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR), granted upon registration. Additionally, processing may rely on contractual necessity (Art. 6.1.b) and/or legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f), appropriately balanced and without prejudice to users' rights.

3. Categories of Personal Data

Only the personal data strictly necessary for the above purposes will be processed, including:

  • Identification data: full name, email address, hospital affiliation.
  • Training data: completed phases, validations, tutor observations, attachments, and comments.

No special categories of data (Art. 9 GDPR) are processed. No automated decision-making or profiling with legal effects is performed (Art. 22 GDPR).

4. Data Recipients and Transfers

Personal data may be disclosed, when necessary for program execution, to:

  • Tutors, CSMs, and professors affiliated with the same hospital as the resident.
  • Participating hospitals, strictly regarding their respective residents.
  • Intuitive Surgical, Inc., if required for training validation or traceability.

No international transfers outside the EEA shall occur unless using technology providers (e.g. MongoDB Atlas, Render, Netlify) that ensure adequate safeguards as per Article 46 GDPR, such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses.

5. Data Retention

Data will be retained for the duration of the training program and for up to one (1) additional year for administrative and statistical purposes, after which it will be securely deleted or irreversibly anonymized (Art. 5.1.e GDPR).

6. User Rights

Users may exercise the rights recognized in Articles 15 to 22 GDPR and Articles 12 to 18 LOPDGDD:

  • Right of Access
  • Right to Rectification
  • Right to Erasure
  • Right to Object
  • Right to Restriction of Processing
  • Right to Data Portability

Requests must be sent to privacy@abexsl.es with valid identification and specification of the right being exercised.

Users may also file a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD).

7. Security Measures

In accordance with Article 32 GDPR, Abex has implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures, including:

  • Encrypted communications (HTTPS)
  • Access control and role-based permissions
  • Activity logging and monitoring
  • Hosting with providers meeting international standards (e.g. ISO 27001, SCC)

8. Amendments to the Privacy Policy

Abex reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy to reflect legislative, jurisprudential, or technical changes. Users will be notified of any significant updates via the Platform or email.
